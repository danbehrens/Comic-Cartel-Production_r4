How and when can I cancel my subscription?

Cancel anytime. 5 minutes after you subscribe, right after you receive your first issue, or after 23 issues. Whenever. Canceling is easy — find the title you’d like to stop receiving and click the “Un-Push” button.

How many premium storage boxes will I get per order?

You must buy $20 worth of comics to get a free storage box. That’s roughly 5 single-issues or 2 trades. Honestly, they’re really expensive for us to make because they’re high quality, so as much as we’d like to, we can’t just give them away unless you order more stuffs. But, if you get over 15, you get another free box. For example, The boxes fit 15-17 comics so if you subscribe to 20 titles and have chosen monthly shipping we’ll send the first 15-17 in one box and the remaining in another at the end of the month.

Do I get my books every month or every week?

It’s your choice! We offer both monthly and weekly shipping. Choose the monthly shipping plan to save a little dough and we’ll ship them to you at the end of every month. If the weekly shipping cost doesn’t bother you, subscribe to that plan and get them every week.

How long will it take to get my comic books?

Depending on where you live typically 2-3 days. Unfortunately, in rare cases, it could take up to 5-7 days due to things such as uncontrollable USPS delays etc. You will always receive a tracking number for each shipment so you can keep an eye on it.

How fast is the speed of light?

Awesome, we love science! The speed of light in a vacuum is 186,282 miles per second (299,792 kilometers per second), and in theory nothing can travel faster than light. In miles per hour, light speed is, well, a lot: about 670,616,629 mph. If you could travel at the speed of light, you could go around the Earth 7.5 times in one second.
